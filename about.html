About Us

We started in 2016 in the high deserts of Albuquerque, New Mexico. We got the idea to make a shirt company that uses locally sourced materials. We started making shirts in our parent's garage and the rest is history. Our motto is to "keep it local". If the job can be provided by a fellow New Mexican, we'l give you our business. We hope that you do the same for us. Enjoy the clothes you wear!


Our business partners include local cotton growers, screenprinting shops, and local vendors that sale our shirts. We can make custom shirts for teams, family reunions, clubs, and businesses. You've got a logo? We get people wearing it!
